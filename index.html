Ian Moon

Game Design and Development Graduate

Rochester Institute of Technology

Hi, I'm Ian.

I'm a 2019 graduate from Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT), with a bachelor's degree in Game Design and Development. I'm currently residing in Nichols, New York. While studying at RIT, I participated in numerous game development projects, both analog and digital, as well as various other coding projects. For digital projects, I have experience in coding web applications in HTML, CSS, Javascript, and NodeJS aswell as making programs in C# utilizing Visual Studio, Unity Engine, and Monogame. For analog games, I've worked on a variety of projects ranging from competitive card games to crafting a tabletop role-playing game. Furthermore, I participated in an internship with funkitron where I worked alongside their level development team on the mobile game Cascade.

Whether I'm working in the virtual world, crafting a tabletop experience, or creating software for non-game reasons there is something that always stays the same. I love the challenge of design and the thrill of implementation. Seeing a project take flight is a really exciting moment for me which has always driven me to take an hands-on approach in terms of management. I like tackling questions of user experience, developing systems, running ideas through the user base, scrapping what didn't work, and going again.

When I'm not coding or designing games, you can find me listening to podcasts, watching educational videos, or playing games with my friends.
