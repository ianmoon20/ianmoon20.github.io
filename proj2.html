Asteroids Remake

Previous Next

Team Size: Personal

Project Type: Arcade Game

Development Tools: Unity

Programming Language: C#

Duration: Not Finished

Description:

Asteroids Remake is a twist on the popular arcade game Asteroids. Achieve higher scores by shooting asteroids and use that score to buy upgrades for the player's ship. Current upgrades are faster movement, faster rotation, and faster reload speed. The amount of incoming asteroids increases with each passing wave.
