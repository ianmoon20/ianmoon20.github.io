Once a Rogue

Team Size: 4

Project Type: Rogue-like RPG

Development Tools: Visual Studio

Programming Language: C#

Duration: 11 Weeks

Description:

Once a Rogue is a procedurally generated rogue-like with RPG elements thrown in the mix. The game contains dungeon exploration aided with a mini-map, advancement through a skill tree, enemy AI and pathfinding, 4 unique sets of skills pertaining to different classes, a combo-system, and a single boss.

My Contributions:

Throughout the development of the game I took an active role in the following aspects: design, development, and leadership.

Design:

  • Game Concept
  • Assisted with player archetypes
  • Assisted with enemies
  • Combo System with skill interactions
  • Final Boss

Development:

  • Player Character
  • Enemy and Player Skills
  • Final Boss
  • Combo System
  • Bug Testing/Debugging
  • Room Builder
    • WPF Application that assisted in creating levels
    • Used to speed up development

Leadership:

  • Assigned workload
